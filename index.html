📋 Free Fire Bio Rules
Know what works and what doesn't before pasting your bio
📏
Character Limit
~50–60 total chars including color codes. [FF0000] = 8 chars. Keep it short.
🎨
Color Format
[FF0000] — UPPERCASE hex in square brackets. No #, no spaces. Lowercase won't work.
🖌️
Max 3 Colors
Maximum 3 different color codes per bio. More than 3 and colors break.
😀
Emojis
[FF0000]🔥Text ✅   [FF0000🔥] ❌ — Emojis must go outside brackets.
👁️
You Won't See Colors
On your own profile you see raw code. Others see colored text. Ask a friend to verify.
⚡
Bold & Italic
[b]Text = bold. [i]Text = italic. Combine: [b][FF0000]ProKing
